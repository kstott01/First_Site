About us

Founded in 2018 we aim to deliver homes away from homes for every family. We are passionate about providing quality homes set upon the beautiful background of Brittany (France). Our homes are handpicked for their charming characters and fully refurbished for your comfort.

Nearby attractions

Explore your surrounding areas whilst staying in the heart of Brittany. Here are some suggestions to get you started...


Vannes

Visit the historic walled city of Vannes...
Founded over 2000 years ago this town is steeped in history. Visit the old town for a range of shops and the harbour to discover fantastic restaurants.

Lake Guerlédan

Or for a more relaxing change of pace, visit Lake Guerlédan...
This artificial lake situated in the middle of Brittany features hiking trails, scenic views and water sports.

Sant-Malo

Follow in the footsteps of pirates...
Once a pirate stronghold, Saint-Malo is now a popular tourist spot boasting a great aquarium perfect for a day out with the kids.
